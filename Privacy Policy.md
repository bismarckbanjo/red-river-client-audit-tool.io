# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how the **Client Audit Tool** handles data.

### Data Access & Purpose
This tool accesses your Google Workspace data on a read-only basis. This access is strictly limited to performing IT onboarding audits to assess operational readiness and technical configuration.

### Data Storage & Security
We prioritize your data privacy. The Client Audit Tool does not store, transmit, or retain any of your Google Workspace data externally. All analysis is performed locally within the scope of your authorized session.
